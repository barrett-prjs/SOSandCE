# Spirits of Steel: Community Edition

Spirits of Steel: Community Edition is a grand strategy war game where you guide a country through diplomacy, industry, politics, military buildup, and war.

## How To Launch

Run `sosandce.exe` from this folder.

## Basic Controls

- `WASD` or arrow keys: move the camera
- Mouse wheel or `[` `]`: zoom the map
- `Space`: open or close the sidebar
- `Esc`: open the pause menu or dismiss popups
- Left click: select provinces, countries, or units
- Left click and drag: box-select multiple divisions
- Right click: move selected divisions

## Main Screens

- Sidebar: politics, military, and industry management
- Top bar: time, economy, and country status
- Focus tree: national focus and long-term planning
- Peace conference: settle wars and choose postwar outcomes

## How To Play

1. Start a country and pause to read your political, military, and industrial situation.
2. Use the sidebar to manage construction, military organization, and national policy.
3. Build your economy so you can support larger armies and longer wars.
4. Move divisions through friendly territory and position them before declaring war.
5. Watch battles, front changes, and map overlays closely as wars develop.
6. Use peace conferences to annex, puppet, demilitarize, or reshape defeated countries.

## Tips

- Check multiple map modes to understand ownership, resources, and industry.
- Keep an eye on manpower, money, and supply-related pressure before expanding wars.
- Use the focus tree early so long-term bonuses start paying off sooner.
- Save often when testing different wars or political paths.
